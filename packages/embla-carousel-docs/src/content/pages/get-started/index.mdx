# Get Started

**Embla Carousel** is a **lightweight**, **dependency-free**, and **framework-agnostic** carousel library. It takes care of the hardest parts of building carousels while leaving full control of markup, styling, and behavior to you. Its extensible [API](/api/) and [plugins](/plugins/) give you the tools to customize it however you need, and it works in all modern browsers.
